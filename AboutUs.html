About Alexander Motors

ALEXANDER MOTORS

Founded in 2000, Alexander Motors is a trusted name in the used car market, located in Arbroath.

We specialize in buying and selling pre-owned vehicles, offering high-quality cars at competitive prices. All our cars come with a 3-month warranty and a 1-year MOT, giving you peace of mind with every purchase.

Additionally, we provide vehicles for spare parts and have a wide selection of parts for sale, making it easy to find what you need for repairs or upgrades.

With over two decades of experience, our team is committed to delivering excellent service. Whether you're looking to purchase a reliable used car or sell your vehicle quickly, Alexander Motors is your go-to destination in Arbroath.
